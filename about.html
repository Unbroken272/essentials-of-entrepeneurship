About SwapNest

Making student moves
easy, affordable and waste-free.

We are a passionate team on a mission to revolutionize how students transition between homes. We know firsthand how stressful, expensive, and wasteful moving out can be. That's why we created a unified, professional platform designed specifically to easily swap furniture, rent starter packs, and book affordable logistics all in one place.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

🌍

Accessibility

Making moving affordable and easy for every student, regardless of their background or budget.

⚙️

Functionality

A seamless, intuitive, one-stop-shop for all your moving needs, from boxes to furniture.

🛡️

Responsibility

Ensuring safe, clean, reliable transactions and upholding the highest standards of trust.

🤝

Collaboration

Powered by the community, emphasizing shared resources and cooperative student networks.

🌱

Sustainability

Committed to reducing furniture waste and carbon footprints through a thriving circular economy.
